Horry County, South Carolina Flood Zones

Map Description

This map consists of 4 Layers.

  • Impact Census HC Addresses: Location of houses that have reported flooding.
  • S_Fld_Haz_AR: Shows Proposed Flood Zones for Horry County.
  • USA Soils Drainage Class: This layer classifies soils in seven classes based on the rate that water moves through the soil.
  • USA NLCD Land Cover: This layer displays a time series of land cover for the United States from the National Land Cover Database (NLCD), at various intervals from 2001 to 2016.

Flood Layers

  • 0.2 PCT ANNUAL CHANCE FLOOD HAZARD: Area of moderate flood hazard, usually the area between the limits of the 100yearand 500 year floodplains.
  • AE: Areas with a 1% annual chance of flooding and a 26% chance of flooding over the life of a 30 year mortgage. Base flood elevations (BFE) and flood hazard factors are determined.
  • X: Areas of minimal flood hazard, determined to be outside the 500 year flood plain.
  • VE: Coastal areas with a 1% or greater chance of flooding and an additional hazard associated with storm waves. These areas have a 26% chance of flooding over the life of a 30 year mortgage. Base flood elevations (BFE) derived from detailed analyses are shown at selected intervals within these zones.
  • A: Areas with a 1% annual chance of flooding and a 26% chance of flooding over the life of a 30 year mortgage. Base flood elevations(BFE) are not available forthese zones.
